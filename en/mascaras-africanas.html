I am a multidisciplinary artist of German and Chilean origin, residing in Peru.

My interest in art led me to participate for years in workshops on history and semiotics of art, painting, sculpture in ceramics and resin.

My recent work has been focused on resin sculpture and African art.

A mask is a medium that communicates the past (spirits, heroes, stories) with the present. It is a guide and an imaginary face for transcendent events or moments.

Who makes them, transmits the spirit from the technique. It is an instrument that connects. Creator and creation, and the one who wears it.

Quien las hace, transmite desde la técnica, el espíritu. Es un instrumento que conecta. Creador y creación, más el que la luce.

It allows a trance and transit, it hides but also exhibits.
